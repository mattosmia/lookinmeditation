About us

'LOOKIN' meditation app has born with the idea to help people to get engaged in a meditative state of mind helping relieve stress and anxiety caused by our routine activities and overthinking.

The application is very simple to use, you just have to download on your phone, sign up and start enjoying the 30 days trial.

The audios available on the app are all guided meditations and are divided into 4 categories: inner peace, love, confidence, and yin-yang. The recorded length takes about 5 to 30 minutes and there is an option to download if you'd like to listen to them on a flight mode for example.

As much more you practice meditation, the better you become, therefore, we suggest you take the advantage of the favorites feature to access your most liked meditations anywhere, anytime.

Enjoy your time meditating with us and always remember to 'look in' yourself.
